What we do?

When you see someone you know, the easiest way to identify them is by their face but not everyone can do this. About 2 percent of our world population is suffering from this condition called Prosopagnosia. Face Assistant is an application developed for three platforms to help people suffering from Prosopagnosia to identify faces quickly.

Google Glass

Just with a single click you see the information of the person. which includes the Name, Your Relationship to that person and the last time you seen that person

Android

Upload an Image of the person you need assistance with and Face Assistant will provide you with the Name, Relationship and Last time you meet

Website

Website works similar to the Android application, upload the image and see the results

Information About Prosopagnosia

Prosopagnosia (also known as 'face blindness') refers to a severe deficit in recognizing familiar people from their face. While some people report a very selective impairment that only influences the recognition of faces, others find the deficit extends to the recognition of other stimuli, such as objects, cars, or animals.

Center for Face Processing Disorders Information About Prospagnosia

Prosopagnosia, also called face blindness, is an impairment in the recognition of faces. It is often accompanied by other types of recognition impairments (place recognition, car recognition, facial expression of emotion, etc.) though sometimes it appears to be restricted to facial identity.

Kinds of Face Blindness

Researchers recognize two types of prosopagnosia—a rare, acquired type, and a more common form called developmental prosopagnosia.
